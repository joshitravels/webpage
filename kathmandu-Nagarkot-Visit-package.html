Itenarary Details:

  • Day 1 Arrive in Kathmandu
    Upon arrival in Kathmandu Airport, you will be greeted and assistance by our Representative. After completing your custom formalities, transfer to Hotel for check-in. Late afternoon visit Boudhanath Stupa, Evening, attend Aarati at Pashupatinath Temple. Drive back to Hotel. Overnight in Kathmandu Hotel.
  • Day 2 Kathmandu
    After breakfast, visit Jalanarayan Temple (Sleeping Vishnu in the water). Later, you will be visiting Swoyambhunath Stupa (Monkey Temple) and Patan Durbar Square and Krishna Temple. Drive back to Hotel. Overnight in Kathmandu Hotel.
  • Day 3: Kathmandu
    Breakfast is at Hotel. Proceed to visit Kailashnath Mahadev Temple (World’s Tallest Shiva Statue) in Saga. Next, you will be visiting Doleshwor Mahadev Temple (Head of Kedarnath) and later, visit Bhaktapur Durbar Square (shooting spot). Nagarkot is 32 km, 1 hr drive from Kathmandu. It is popular for enjoying Himalayan views and sunrise, sunset from the comfort of your hotel balcony. Arrive and check in the hotel. Evening, sunset and Himalayan view (subject to weather condition). Overnight in Nagarkot Hotel.
  • Day 4Kathmandu - Departure
    After breakfast, free till departure. Later, on time transfer to International Airport for onward destination.
